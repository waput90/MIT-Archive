# Privacy and Compliance

## Applicable Law

This project is intended to comply with Philippine privacy law, including:
- Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012).
- Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR).
- National Privacy Commission issuances, where applicable.

## Voice Data Risk Notice

Voice data may be personally identifiable. Contributors and maintainers must treat audio/transcript records as potentially sensitive personal data.

## Voice Cloning Prohibition

Repository dataset materials must not be used by the public to clone, synthesize, or impersonate contributor voices.
This includes training or deploying speaker-cloning TTS, voice conversion, or related identity-mimicking systems for contributed speakers.

## Minimum Compliance Expectations

For maintainers and contributors handling data:
- Obtain informed consent before recording/collection.
- Collect only data necessary for the research objective.
- Keep contributor identity/contact details separate from public dataset releases when feasible.
- Avoid publishing unnecessary personal identifiers.
- Remove data when legal or consent obligations require removal.

## Consent Requirement

Use the repository consent form and process before accepting contributor data:
- Voice_Recording_Transcript_Consent_Form.docx
- CONSENT_CHECKLIST.md

## Incident Response

If you discover privacy, consent, or rights issues in a contributed sample, escalate immediately and pause redistribution of affected files until reviewed.
