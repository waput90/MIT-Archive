# Contributing

## Scope Rules

This main repository accepts contributions for:
- Davao Cebuano language data and related code-switching work.
- Whisper model-based ASR pipelines.

Fork required when:
- You are focusing on Philippine languages other than Davao Cebuano.
- You are switching to a non-Whisper ASR model family.

## Required Before Data Contribution

Contributors must complete all of the following before audio/transcript submission:
- Read README.md, DATA_LICENSE.md, and PRIVACY_AND_COMPLIANCE.md.
- Review and sign Voice_Recording_Transcript_Consent_Form.docx.
- Confirm understanding that contributed data is for free non-commercial research/education use only.
- Confirm understanding that commercial use of contributed dataset material is prohibited in this repository.
- Confirm understanding that public voice cloning, speaker impersonation, or voice synthesis of contributed speakers is prohibited.
- Confirm compliance with Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012) and related rules.

## Contributor Certification

By submitting a contribution, you certify that:
- You have rights to contribute the content.
- You have secured valid informed consent for any identifiable voice data.
- You will not use repository dataset materials to build, train, or distribute voice-cloning or speaker-impersonation systems for contributed voices.
- Your contribution complies with repository scope, data license, and privacy requirements.

## Maintainer Rights

Maintainers may reject or remove contributions that do not meet consent, licensing, privacy, or scope requirements.
